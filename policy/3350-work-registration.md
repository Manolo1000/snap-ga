# Section 3350 — Work Registration

Source: Georgia DFCS SNAP Policy Manual (MT-87, June 2026)
URL: https://pamms.dhs.ga.gov/dfcs/snap/3350/

---

Most SNAP recipients aged 16 through 59 must register for work as a condition of eligibility, unless exempt. Work registration means being available for and accepting suitable employment.

## Exemptions from work registration

An individual is exempt from work registration if they are:
- Under 16 or 60 or older
- Physically or mentally unfit for employment
- Already working at least 30 hours per week (or earning weekly wages equal to at least 30 hours × federal minimum wage)
- Receiving unemployment compensation or has applied
- Participating in a drug or alcohol treatment program
- Responsible for a dependent child under 6, or for an incapacitated person
- Complying with TANF work requirements
- A student enrolled at least half-time in school (recognized institution)
- Age 16 or 17 and not the head of household
- A migrant or seasonal farmworker under contract to begin work within 30 days

## Consequences of failure to comply

If a non-exempt individual refuses without good cause to register for work, accept a suitable job offer, or comply with a work requirement:
- The individual is disqualified from SNAP
- The disqualification period is 1 month for a first violation, 3 months for a second, and 6 months for a third and subsequent violations
- The rest of the household remains eligible; the disqualified member's income is still counted (in whole or in part depending on 3625/3635 budgeting)

## Good cause

Good cause exceptions include: illness, illness of another household member requiring the individual's presence, household emergency, lack of transportation, lack of adequate child care, unfit working conditions, discrimination, and other verifiable circumstances beyond the individual's control.

## Relationship to ABAWD (3355)

Work registration is a separate requirement from the ABAWD work requirement. An ABAWD must both register for work under 3350 AND meet the 80-hour monthly work requirement under 3355 (unless exempt from either).
